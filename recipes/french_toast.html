French Toast

Description

This recipe comes from Bonnie on Allrecipes and uses flour to ensure a fluffy end product!

Ingredients

  • 1/4th cup all-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Ground cinnamon
  • A pinch of salt
  • Olive oil, for cooking
  • 12 slices of thick-sliced bread (brioche, sourdough, challah, etc.)
  • Maple syrup, powdered sugar, and fresh fruit, for topping

Directions

  1. Slowly whisk the milk into the flour, then whisk in the remaining ingredients.
  2. Saturate the bread slices in the batter.
  3. Cook the French toast on a lightly oiled griddle or pan until bread is golden brown on both sides.
  4. Top with maple syrup, powdered sugar, and fresh fruit.
