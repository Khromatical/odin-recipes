Double Chocolate Mint Cookies

Description

This recipe comes from Paula Jo on Allrecipes and is a simple treat your whole family can enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/2 cups butter, softened
  • 4 cups white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon peppermint extract
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Cream together 2 and 1/2 cups butter, 4 eggs, and 4 cups white sugar in a bowl. Then, mix the rest of the ingredients in evenly.
  3. Drop mixture by teaspoon onto a cookie sheet, then bake for 8-9 minutes. Cookies should be soft.
  4. Cool for about a minute on a cookie sheet, then transfer cookies to a wire rack to completely cool.
